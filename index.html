About US

Eyebrow Queen Salon is a new and upcoming complete salon founded in 2012. Dedicated to making you look your best, with years of experience we offer the best of the best employees at your service. Our Services


Facial

A facial is a beauty treatment for your face. We use everything from oils, creams, gels and massage to give you cleaner, healthier, brighter skin.

Threading

Threading is an ancient hair-removal technique, practiced for centuries among the most beautiful women of Asia and the Middle East. It is the best alternative to waxing and tweezing, especially for sensitive skin.

Eyelash Extension

Give your eyelashes a makeover by our exclusive eyelash extension service. You’ll love the way your eyes’ll look

Eyebrow/Eyelash Tinting

Tinting is perfectly safe for the eye area – you can even tint your eyelashes! The tint is a vegetable dye mixed with diluted peroxide. The tint is left on for anywhere from 1-5 minutes, depending on how dark you want your eyebrows

Henna Tattoo

Beautiful impermanent tattoos made out of henna. Henna is known locally throughout the Middle East and North Africa as ‘Henna’ or ‘Hene’ . It is also known as ‘Mendhi’.

Waxing

Waxing removes hair from the root, which is why it lasts longer than shaving or depilatory creams. Hair removal can last anything from three to eight weeks, depending on the individual’s hair growth cycle.
